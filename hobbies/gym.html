Gym

I enjoy going to the gym because it is one of the most direct ways to improve yourself. It is simple, structured and based on consistency over time.

There are no shortcuts. You either show up and put in the work or you don’t. Over time, the results start to build up naturally.

What I Like About It

  • Clear and measurable progress, although not linear at all
  • Builds discipline and consistency
  • Improves both mental and physical strength
  • Helps with routine and structure
  • Rewards long-term effort

Small improvements over time add up more than people expect. A lesson from a favorite book - Atomic Habits

For me, the gym is a way to stay balanced and keep improving in a very direct way.
