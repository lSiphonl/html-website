Why I find our brain interesting

The human brain is one of nature's most remarkable feats of evultion and an magnificent example of a complex system engineered to solve problems. I beleive every single person has the capabilities to understand anything given enough time and effort. Not only can you make it overpowered by leveraging neuroplasticity, but you can literally turn it into a weapon of great power, but always remember what Peter Parker's uncle said - "With great power comes great responsibility".

What makes it so interesting?

Well, simply put

  • It's the most complex system in the known universe
  • It has the ability to learn and adapt, which is a superpower in itself
  • It is the source of our consciousness, thoughts, emotions, and creativity
  • It has the potential to unlock the mysteries of existence and solve problems we haven't even thought of yet
  • It governs our will, wishes and ambitions
  • It very much follows the GIGO rule, or "You are what you eat"

It truly is but a masterpiece, the greatest combination of atoms and molecules working together in an intecrate system to create something above our understanding. The ability of the brain to be able to study itself is incredible.

I am a very introspective person who enjoys spending time in my own thoughts, I believe with all my heart that spending time in your own head, analysing your thoughts, thought patterns, and emotions is one of the best ingredients to fullfilling your potential.
