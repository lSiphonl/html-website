Reading

I enjoy reading because it gives me access to ideas, systems and perspectives that I would not normally encounter in day to day life. It is one of the simplest ways to learn, but also one of the most powerful if you approach it with curiosity.

I tend to read a wide range of material depending on what interests me at the time. This includes self-improvement books, story based books, science books and even textbooks on subjects I find interesting. Sometimes I will even pick up a calculus book or something technical just to understand how a concept works at a deeper level.

What I Read

  • Self-improvement books
  • Story and fiction books
  • Science and technical books
  • Textbooks on topics that interest me

I like reading things that challenge the way I think, not just entertain me.

Books I Have Enjoyed

  • Atomic Habits
  • 48 Laws of Power
  • A Song of Ice and Fire

These books stand out to me because they each explore different systems of thinking, whether it is habits, human behaviour or complex fictional worlds.

Reading is something I use both for learning and curiosity. It helps me explore ideas at my own pace and build a deeper understanding of topics that I find meaningful.
