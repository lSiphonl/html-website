What do I mean by Planning and Organising?

Well, "planning things" pretty much means what it says, but organising complexity can have a subjective definition, so I'll share my view and why I have an interest in it.

My view and interest on organising complexity:

  • I calssify organising "complexity" in terms of systems
  • Where the system is anything which is messy and chaotic
  • I firmly believe in the law of entropy and have incorporated it into learning and work - I realised that not only does this law apply to large bodies of mass in our universe, but also our work, if we don't have a complete understanding of the fundamentals then a system will turn messy and continue to get messier over time, making more advanced components even more difficult to understand. (Something i picked up in software project management)
My views indirectly explain the reason for my interest in organising things, I love taking chaos and filtering, abstracting, remodelling it to get clarity on a subject. This has helped me countlessly over the course of my software career and even more in terms of picking up new hobbies, interests and skills.
